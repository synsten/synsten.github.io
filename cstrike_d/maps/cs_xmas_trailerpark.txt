Trailerpark X-Mas Edition - Hostage Rescue
by Cmdr

Based on cs_trailerpark by Chris Auty

Textures by Cmdr, 3DMike, Running with Scissors
Music by Leroy Anderson

Evil terrorists took hostages in an old trailerpark
during christmas day. Unfortunatly, a blizzard
occurs once the Counter-Terrorists arrive.

Counter-Terrorists: Enter the old 
trailerpark to rescue the hostages.
Take out the Terrorists without jeopardizing 
the hostages. Thank God we brought some
sledges to progress through the snow.

Terrorists: Prevent Counter-Terrorist
force from rescuing the hostages.

Other Notes: There are 4 hostages in 
the mission.
